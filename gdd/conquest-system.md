# Conquest & Dominion System

## Purpose
Allows powerful players to take control of camps, towns, castles, airships, and vessels either for a king or for their own kingdom.

## Required Rank

| Location Type | Required Rank |
|---|---|
| Bandit Camp | Knight |
| Resource Camp | Knight |
| Village | Lord |
| Town | Baron |
| Castle | Duke |
| Airship | Captain / Duke |
| Naval Vessel | Captain / Duke |
| Kingdom Capital | King / Emperor |

## Ownership Types

- Neutral
- Player Owned
- Kingdom Owned
- Enemy Owned
- Faction Controlled

## Capture Rules

A location can be captured if:

- Player has the required rank
- Enemy defenders are defeated
- Player has enough influence
- Player is not banned from the region
- The location is not protected by story rules

## Rewards

Capturing territory can provide:

- Gold income
- Troop recruitment
- Resources
- Trade access
- Strategic control
- Fast travel points
- Political influence

## Airship & Vessel Capture

Players may capture airships and ships:

- In service of a king
- For their own kingdom
- As pirates or rebels
- For trade and military transport

## World Reaction

Taking land increases threat level.

Consequences:

- Enemy retaliation
- Rival lords declaring war
- Bounty hunters
- Rebellions
- Diplomatic penalties
