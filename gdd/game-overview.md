# The Realm: Rise of Empires

## Vision

Create an open-world fantasy RPG where players can rise from a common adventurer to become rulers of kingdoms, dragon riders, powerful mages, merchants, or dark lords.

## Core Gameplay

- Exploration
- Combat
- Questing
- Kingdom Building
- Dragon Riding
- Diplomacy
- Trading
- Warfare

## Target Engine

Unreal Engine 5

## Target Platform

PC

## Current Development Stage

Prototype V1

## Prototype Goal

Create a playable world featuring:

- Third Person Character
- Combat
- Inventory
- Merchant NPC
- Quest System
- Village
- Castle
- Forest
- Cave

## Success Condition

Player completes first quest and earns the title of Knight.
