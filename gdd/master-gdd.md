# The Realm: Rise of Empires

## Vision

A massive open-world fantasy RPG where player decisions shape the world.

Players may become:

- Adventurers
- Knights
- Lords
- Kings
- Wizards
- Dragon Riders
- Airship Captains
- Merchants
- Mercenaries
- Dark Lords

## Core Pillars

### Freedom
Choose your own path.

### Progression
Grow from a nobody into a world-changing leader.

### Kingdoms
Build, defend and expand your lands.

### Dragons
Tame, ride and command dragons.

### Magic
Study at Cloudspire Academy.

### Warfare
Lead armies and conquer territory.

### Diplomacy
Form alliances and shape politics.

### Exploration
Discover ancient ruins, relics and hidden powers.

## End Goal

Create a living fantasy world that continues evolving through player actions.
