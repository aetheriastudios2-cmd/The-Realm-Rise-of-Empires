# Factions of Aetheria

## Kingdom of Men
The central human kingdom built on castles, knights, lords, trade, and noble houses.

## Ashborn Empire
A volcanic empire forged in fire, discipline, dragon hunting, and conquest.

## Sylvan Dominion
Ancient forest civilisation connected to nature, spirits, elves, and living cities.

## Frostclans
Northern warrior clans who survive in frozen lands and value strength, loyalty, and honour.

## Skyward Confederacy
Airship captains, sky sailors, and dragon hunters living among the floating hills of the west.

## Umbral Dominion
A hidden dark faction unlocked through the Shadow Crown questline. It allows the player to become a Dark Lord and command undead forces.

## Orc Tribes
Powerful cave-dwelling warriors who live by strength, conquest, and clan rule.

## Goblin Clans
Cunning underground traders, raiders, inventors, and ambushers.

## Troll Clans
Ancient cave giants used as living siege engines.
