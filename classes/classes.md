# Player Paths

## Warrior
Master of melee combat.

## Knight
Noble warrior serving kingdoms.

## Lord
Controls land, armies and settlements.

## King
Rules an entire kingdom.

## Mercenary
Works for gold and contracts.

## Wizard
Graduates from Cloudspire Academy.

## Dragon Rider
Forms a bond with a living dragon.

## Airship Captain
Commands flying fleets.

## Dark Lord
Unlocks through the Shadow Crown questline.

## Merchant Prince
Builds wealth through trade.

## Adventurer
Free roaming explorer.
