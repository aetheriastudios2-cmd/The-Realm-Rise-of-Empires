# Kingdom and Land System

## Noble Progression
Players can rise through titles:

- Knight
- Baron
- Count
- Duke
- King
- Emperor

## Land Ownership
With higher titles, players can own and develop land.

Players may build:

- Manors
- Mansions
- Castles
- Villages
- Towns
- Cities
- Airship docks
- Mage towers
- Dragon roosts

## Responsibilities
Owning land means protecting people.

Threats include:

- Bandits
- Orc raids
- Goblin ambushes
- Troll attacks
- Enemy armies
- Dragons
- Undead invasions

## Founder Kingdom
Players can create their own kingdom with:

- Custom name
- Custom banner
- Unique troop tree
- Laws
- Nobles
- Castles
- Dynasties

## World Conquest
Rulers can conquer land, rebuild ruined regions, defend citizens, expand borders, and shape the world.
