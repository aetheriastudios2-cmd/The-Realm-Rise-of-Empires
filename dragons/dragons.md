# Dragons of Aetheria

## Dragon Bond System
Once the player unlocks dragon connection, they may form bonds with dragons and add selected dragons to their army.

Power attracts danger. As the player gains dragons, enemies become stronger and more organised.

## Aurelion
The largest living dragon after Morvakar. A legendary ancient dragon hidden in the temple of the Ancient Order. Can be tamed only by a true Dragon Master Rider.

## Morvakar
The biggest legendary dragon in existence. A dead dark dragon awakened only through the final Dark Lord ability.

## Dragon Types
- Fire Dragons
- Frost Dragons
- Storm Dragons
- Forest Dragons
- Shadow Dragons
- Sea Dragons
- Celestial Dragons

## Dragon Rules
- Lesser dragons can join armies.
- Elder dragons require rare quests.
- Legendary dragons are unique.
- Dragon Kings change the fate of the world.
