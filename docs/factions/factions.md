# Factions of The Realm: Rise of Empires

## Kingdom of Men
A noble civilization of knights, kings, lords, merchants, and dragon riders.

## Ashborn Empire
A volcanic military empire forged through discipline and conquest.

## Sylvan Dominion
Ancient forest dwellers who protect nature and master powerful magic.

## Frostclans
Hardy northern warriors who thrive in frozen lands.

## Skyward Confederacy
Airship captains and dragon hunters who defend the floating western hills.

## Umbral Dominion
A hidden faction unlocked through the Shadow Crown questline.
Players may become the Dark Lord and command undead armies.

## Orc Tribes
Warlike clans inhabiting mountains and caves.

## Goblin Clans
Cunning traders, inventors, and raiders of the underground.

## Troll Clans
Ancient giants dwelling beneath the world.

## Neutral Powers

### Mage Academy
Floating temple in the clouds where players can become powerful wizards.

### Dragon Orders
Ancient groups dedicated to studying and taming dragons.
