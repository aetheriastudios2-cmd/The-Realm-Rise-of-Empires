# World Overview

## The Realm

A vast continent divided between kingdoms, empires, tribes, and ancient powers.

---

## Major Regions

### Lion's Reach
Heartland of the Kingdom of Men.

### Ashfall Dominion
Volcanic homeland of the Ashborn Empire.

### Sylvanwood
Ancient forests of the elves.

### Frostpeak North
Frozen lands of the Frostclans.

### Skyward Hills
Floating western islands protected by the Skyward Confederacy.

### The Deep Warrens
Cave systems inhabited by Orcs, Goblins and Trolls.

### Shadowlands
Corrupted lands surrounding the Dark Temples.

### Dragonspine Mountains
Ancient dragon nesting grounds.

### Cloudspire Academy
Floating Mage Academy hidden among the clouds.
