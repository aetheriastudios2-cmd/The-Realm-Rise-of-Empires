# Join Aetheria Studios

We are building The Realm: Rise of Empires, an ambitious open-world fantasy RPG and kingdom simulator in Unreal Engine 5.

## Current Stage
Pre-production and worldbuilding.

## Looking For
- Unreal Engine 5 Developers
- Blueprint Developers
- C++ Developers
- Environment Artists
- Character Artists
- Creature Artists
- Animators
- Writers
- Sound Designers
- Concept Artists

## Compensation
This is currently a volunteer / hobby / rev-share project.

There is no payment available at this stage.

If the project later receives funding, backing, publishing support, crowdfunding, or revenue, contributors may be paid or included through agreed revenue-share terms.

No future payment is guaranteed.

## Goal
Build a playable prototype of The Realm: Rise of Empires, beginning with one region, one kingdom, combat, quests, and recruitment.
